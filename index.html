Hello, I'm Akhil Mistri, a 20-year-old artist with an insatiable love for bringing portraits to life through the meticulous use of graphite, charcoal, and colored pencils.
The allure of realism lies in the ability to evoke emotions through my art. I am captivated by the subtle nuances of human features, the play of light on a face, and the stories that unfold within every gaze. As I work on my drawings, I strive to transcend the medium, seeking to breathe life into the paper and connect viewers to the souls captured within the lines.
